My art process

I do get asked how do i create such a hot garbage.. well idk where you want me to be counted to digital or tradictional since my process is extreamly speciall.. JUST LIKE ME :DD

The first thing i do for my art is draw on paper, quick little doodle. Then i take picture of it and upload to my pc.

Afterwards i open gimp and grayscale it so lines show. i ussualy from start do little bit of contrast then linear desaturation and then more contrast and then posterization to get those nice looking half shadows :DD

Then i set the layer to multiply and color in using gimps pen tool (dont use brusshes XD) or lasso tool.. OR i just use bunch of effects if i dont need coloring :DD
